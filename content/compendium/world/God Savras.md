Savras is the lawful neutral god of divination, fate, and truth.

**Titles:** The All-Seeing, The Diviner, Lord of Divination.
**Symbol:** A crystal ball containing many eyes.
**Domains:** Knowledge, Order.
**Portfolio:** Divination, foresight, truth, fate, and the revealing of lies.

# Nature

Savras represents structure within prophecy. He sees all outcomes but rarely intervenes, valuing accuracy over mercy. His followers view reality as a grand equation of cause and effect, with divine sight as the means to read it.

# Dogma

- Seek truth in all things.
- Use divination responsibly.
- Knowledge is sacred only when shared in order.
- Fate can be read but not rewritten.

# Worship

Temples are few. Shrines often appear in libraries, observatories, and wizard towers. Worshippers include diviners, oracles, astrologers, and judges who prize truth over comfort.
